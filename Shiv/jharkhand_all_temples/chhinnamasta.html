Main Content

The Chhinnamasta Temple, dedicated to Goddess Chhinnamasta, is located at the confluence of the Damodar and Bhairavi rivers in Rajrappa, Jharkhand. The temple is an important Shakti Peetha and is renowned for its tantric rituals and unique iconography where the goddess is depicted holding her severed head. It attracts thousands of devotees during major Hindu festivals and is especially significant among tantric practitioners.
