Main Content

Baidyanath Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, is a highly revered pilgrimage destination in India. According to legend, Ravana brought the Shiva lingam here, but it was placed on the ground and became immovable, leading to the establishment of the temple. The temple, built in the Nagara style by Raja Puran Mal, attracts millions during the Shravani Mela.
