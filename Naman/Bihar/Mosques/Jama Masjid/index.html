Jama masjid (Patna)

Main Offering of Jama masjid (Patna)

  • Namaz (Salah): Daily prayers for the Muslim community.
  • Jumu'ah (Friday Prayer): Special congregational prayer every Friday.
  • Festive Prayers: Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha special prayers.
  • Nikah Services: Islamic marriage ceremonies conducted at the mosque.

Major Festivals at Jama masjid (Patna)

  • Eid-ul-Fitr: Large gatherings for special prayers mark the end of Ramadan. The mosque is filled with devotees offering namaz in the morning.
  • Eid-ul-Adha: Special prayers and community gatherings are held to celebrate the festival of sacrifice.
  • Jumma (Friday Prayers): Every Friday, the mosque hosts significant congregational prayers, especially during festivals like Holi when people from different communities join in the spirit of harmony.

Jama Masjid is a symbol of Patna's cultural and spiritual heritage, welcoming people during major Islamic festivals and fostering communal harmony.

Jama Masjid Patna stands as one of the most prominent and historic mosques in Bihar, located in the bustling New Market area of Patna City. Renowned for its exquisite Mughal architecture, the mosque captivates visitors with its grand domes, intricate carvings, and expansive courtyards that offer a tranquil retreat from the city’s vibrant life.

Built in the early 18th century, Jama Masjid is not only a place of worship but also a center of community and culture. The mosque's impressive façade, adorned with beautiful arches and minarets, reflects the artistic brilliance of its era. During major Islamic festivals like Eid and Ramadan, thousands of devotees gather here, filling the air with a sense of unity and devotion.

  • Location: New Market, Patna City, Bihar
  • Architectural Style: Mughal
  • Key Features: Grand domes, ornate minarets, spacious prayer halls, and a serene courtyard
  • Significance: Major center for religious gatherings, community events, and nikah (marriage) ceremonies

Whether you are a devotee, a history enthusiast, or a traveler, Jama Masjid Patna offers a glimpse into the rich spiritual and architectural legacy of Bihar. Its peaceful ambiance and cultural vibrancy make it a must-visit destination in the heart of Patna.
